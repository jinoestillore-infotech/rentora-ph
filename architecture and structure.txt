RENTORA PH: Web-Based Online Boarding House Finder and Management System Using Role-Based Access Control

RENTORA PH is a web-based platform designed to simplify 
the process of finding, renting, and managing boarding 
houses. The system connects tenants with boarding house 
owners through an online marketplace where available rooms 
can be searched, viewed, and applied for conveniently.

The platform implements Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) 
to provide specific functionalities for three user roles: 
Admin, Owner (Boarding House Owner), and Tenant. Administrators 
oversee the entire system, boarding house owners manage 
their properties and tenants, while tenants can search for 
boarding houses, submit rental applications, and make 
online rent payments.

By centralizing property listings, tenant management, 
and rental transactions into a single platform, 
RENTORA PH aims to improve accessibility, efficiency, 
and communication between boarding house owners and tenants.


Roles:



1. Admin

- Manage all users

- Approve boarding house listings

- Manage reports and complaints

- View system analytics



2. Boarding House Owner

- Register boarding houses

- Add rooms and prices

- Upload photos

- Approve/reject tenant applications

- Track payments

- Manage tenants



3. Tenant

- Register account

- Search boarding houses

- Filter by location, price, amenities

- View room details and photos

- Apply/book a room

- Pay rent

- Submit maintenance requests
